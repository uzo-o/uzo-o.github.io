SYNOPSIS:

Skam (English: Shame) is a Norwegian teen drama web series about the daily life of students at the Hartvig Nissen . From 2015 to 2017, a new main character was followed each season. While airing, a new clip, text message conversation, or social media post was published in real-time and displayed at skam.p3.no on a daily basis. Each season has focus on particular topics, ranging from relationship difficulties, identity, eating disorders, sexual assault, homosexuality, mental health issues, religion, and forbidden love.
