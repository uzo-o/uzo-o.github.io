[SYNOPSIS]

Skam (English: Shame) is a Norwegian teen drama series about the daily life of students attending Hartvig Nissen School. From 2015 to 2017, a new main character was followed each season. While airing, a new clip, text message conversation, or social media post from the perspective of the protagonist was published in real-time and displayed at skam.p3.no on a nearly daily basis. Each season focuses on a particular subset of cultural issues relevant to its given character, ranging from self-image and relationship issues to homosexuality and religion.
